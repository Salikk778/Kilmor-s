Activities

Water Sports

Water Sports

  • Kayaking: Paddle through the serene waters of Bhimtal.
  • Paddle Boating: Relax with family and friends on our paddle boats for a fun day on the lake.
  • Water Zorbing: Experience the thrill of walking inside a large inflatable ball on the water's surface—fun for all ages!

Hot Air Balloon

Hot Air Balloon

Hot Air Ballooning in Bhimtal offers breathtaking views of the town and Himalayan ranges. Enjoy 12-15 minutes in the air at around 100 feet, perfect for capturing stunning photos. This activity is ideal for both morning and evening, showcasing beautiful sunrise and sunset vistas.

Paragliding

Paragliding

Bhimtal is very popular for paragliding. The experience starts from near the Bhimtal Lake, a spot that can be reached after an easy trek of around 15 minutes. Fly towards Naukuchiatal and enjoy grand bird's eye views of the magnificent valley.

Camping

Camping

Just 30 minutes away from Bhimtal is the small village of Padampuri, a very popular camping site. Surrounded by forests, teeming with a variety of flora and fauna and with the Kala river flowing by, a variety of camping options are available here.

Trekking

Trekking

Bhimtal offers several trekking options. Take a 3 km hike to Karkotaka Mandir for stunning views of Bhimtal, Naukuchiyatal, and nearby areas, or enjoy a 2 km trek to Hidimba Parvat and its green surroundings. You can also hike to a nearby ridge for a partial view of Sattal Lake.

Stargazing

Stargazing

Bhimtal's clear skies and low light pollution make it perfect for stargazing. On a clear night, you can enjoy views of countless stars and even spot the Milky Way. For a more immersive experience, visit the nearby Stargate Observatory, just 3 km from Bhimtal Lake, which offers astronomy shows and night sky programs.
